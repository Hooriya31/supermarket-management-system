# Supermarket-system
Project Overview
This project presents a Comprehensive Inventory & Sales Management System designed to streamline business operations through automated product tracking, customer relationship management, and real-time sales analytics.

Problem Statement
Small to medium-sized businesses often struggle with:
Manual inventory tracking leading to stock discrepancies
Inefficient customer data management
Lack of real-time sales insights
Time-consuming manual report generation
Poor data persistence and recovery mechanisms


Solution
Our Java-based application addresses these challenges by providing:
Automated Inventory Management: Real-time stock tracking with low-stock alerts
Customer Relationship Management: Complete customer lifecycle management
Sales Processing Engine: Efficient cart-based checkout system
Advanced Analytics: Comprehensive reporting and business intelligence
Robust Data Persistence: File-based storage with exceptional handling
Secure Authentication: Role-based access control system


Conclusion
The Inventory & Sales Management System successfully achieves its goal of streamlining business operations for small to medium-sized enterprises. By automating product tracking, customer management, and sales analytics, the system reduces manual errors, improves data accuracy, and provides actionable business insights in real time.
Through a well-structured division of tasks among team members, each module—from Product & Inventory Management, Customer & Sales Transactions, to Analytics & Reporting—was efficiently implemented with robust Java programming concepts. The system’s secure authentication, intuitive menus, and comprehensive reporting dashboard ensure both usability and reliability.
